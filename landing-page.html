HOW IT WORKS
Improve your meetings, improve your time. With Sortello everyone can participate in prioritizing, bringing pragmatic solutions.
STEP 1
Download sortello from the chrome store and install the extension on your browser
STEP 2
Go to your Trello board, choose the column to be prioritized and click on the sortello's button
STEP 3
Sortello compares the cards in order to facilitate prioritization
STEP 4
Send the ordered cards and look at the prioritization result immediately
WHY IT WORKS
Deciding which one out of two tasks has higher priority, is easier than having to sort a list of n tasks at once.
